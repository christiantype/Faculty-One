What Faculty does

Faculty is a graphic design studio that cares. It was founded upon the idea that design ought to improve the human condition. Through partnerships with organizations and institutions focused on doing good, Faculty aims to communicate and produce meaningful executions. Even when not engaged in client work, Faculty is engaged in research and relationships across disciplines and industries.

What Faculty means

Definitions

  1. an inherent mental or physical power.
  2. a group of university departments concerned with a major division of knowledge.

In today’s society of consumption, our faculties are often not included in daily discourse. Distraction threatens our abilities to thrive, so Faculty’s focus is to encourage our faculties to manifest through the arts. Faculty understands the power of collective community and the importance of diversity of thought, which is why we emphasize Faculty, as a division of knowledge that mindfully approaches problems through the a speculative lens of design that considers different outcomes and prioritizes the well-being of society, community, and people.
