Founder

Christian Solorzano

Christian is a designer, writer, principal, and creative director at Faculty. He received a Bachelor's Degree in Graphic Design from DePaul University in 2016, and has experience working with non-profits, educational institutions, advertising, and corporate clients across the country. Christian founded Faculty due to his passion for design and community.
